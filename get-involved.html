Get Involved

1. Sponsor a Legal Awareness & Training Camp

Support the expansion of our legal literacy and constitutional awareness programmes across India.

Sponsoring a training camp enables citizens to understand their legal rights, recognize unlawful practices, and take informed, lawful action against violations. These camps focus on constitutional safeguards, statutory compliance, and citizen responsibilities.

2. Make a Financial Contribution (Classified by Purpose)

Financial contributions to the Legal Rights Protection Forum directly strengthen our capacity to operate independently and effectively.

Contributions may be made under the following classified activity heads:

  • Legal Action & Litigation Support:
    To support public interest litigation, statutory complaints, legal notices, and court-related expenses in matters involving constitutional violations, misuse of public or foreign funds, and unlawful activities.
  • Research & Documentation:
    To fund in-depth legal research, fact-finding investigations, compilation of evidence, preparation of reports, and policy submissions to competent authorities.
  • Legal Awareness & Citizen Empowerment:
    To support training programmes, workshops, publications, and educational materials aimed at empowering citizens with legal knowledge.
  • Operational & Compliance Costs:
    To meet essential administrative, documentation, and compliance-related expenses necessary for sustaining lawful and transparent operations.

Every contribution—large or small—strengthens the rule of law and advances accountability.

3. Become a Whistleblower

If you possess credible information relating to corruption, misuse of foreign contributions, financial irregularities, or activities that may threaten India’s constitutional, legal, or national security framework, we provide a secure and confidential platform for responsible disclosure.

Whistleblower identities are protected to the fullest extent permitted by law.

4. Partner with Us

We welcome collaboration with individuals, legal professionals, researchers, civil society organisations, and institutions that share our commitment to constitutional values, transparency, and accountability.

Partnerships may include knowledge-sharing, joint research, training initiatives, or institutional support.

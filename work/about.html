About Us

The Legal Rights Protection Forum (LRPF) is a non-profit organization dedicated to upholding India's constitutional framework, national security, and legal integrity. We are a team of passionate legal and social activists, lawyers, and researchers who operate on the frontlines of legal activism.

Our Mission: Our mission is to build a lawful, transparent, and secure India by holding organizations and individuals accountable for violations of national law. We believe in empowering citizens with the knowledge and tools they need to protect their rights and contribute to a more just and secure society.

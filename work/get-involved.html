Get Involved

1. Sponsor a Camp

Support the expansion of our legal literacy and constitutional awareness programmes across India. Sponsoring a training camp enables citizens to understand their legal rights, recognize unlawful practices, and take informed, lawful action against violations. These camps focus on constitutional safeguards, statutory compliance, and citizen responsibilities.

2. Make a Financial Contribution

Financial contributions directly strengthen our capacity to operate independently. Contributions may be made under the following heads:

  • Legal Action & Litigation Support: For PILs, complaints, and court expenses.
  • Research & Documentation: For investigations and reports.
  • Legal Awareness: For workshops and educational materials.
  • Operational Costs: For administrative and compliance expenses.

4. Partner with Us

We welcome collaboration with individuals, legal professionals, researchers, civil society organisations, and institutions that share our commitment to constitutional values, transparency, and accountability. Partnerships may include knowledge-sharing, joint research, training initiatives, or institutional support.

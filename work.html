Our Work

At the Legal Rights Protection Forum (LRPF), our work is centered on addressing critical issues that affect India's legal, social, and national fabric. Our dedicated team of experts focuses on the following key areas to ensure accountability and uphold the rule of law.Through strategic legal action, public awareness campaigns, and investigative reporting, LRPF works to expose and prevent activities that threaten India's sovereignty and its citizens' rights.

Child Rights

We are dedicated to the protection of children and their fundamental rights. Our efforts focus on combating issues such as child labor, trafficking, and abuse. We work to ensure that legal provisions designed to protect children are effectively enforced, and we advocate for policies that create a safer environment for them to grow and thrive.

Human Rights

LRPF is committed to upholding the basic human rights guaranteed to every individual under the Constitution of India and international law. We take action against violations of human rights, including discrimination, unlawful detention, and social injustice. Our work involves legal intervention and public advocacy to ensure that justice is served and that the dignity of every citizen is respected.

Financial Accountability & FCRA

We are committed to ensuring financial transparency, especially concerning foreign funding. We actively investigate and file formal complaints with government authorities regarding the misuse of foreign funds. Our work targets violations of the Foreign Contribution (Regulation) Act (FCRA), a crucial law that regulates the acceptance and utilization of foreign contributions by individuals and associations in India. By monitoring compliance and reporting violations, we help prevent these funds from being used for purposes that are detrimental to India's interests.

National Security

National security is a cornerstone of our work. We closely monitor suspicious activities that are backed by foreign entities and have the potential to destabilize the nation. By highlighting these threats to the appropriate authorities and the public, we aim to prevent actions that could compromise India's sovereignty and internal security. Our focus is on proactive intervention to counter foreign-backed agendas that pose a risk to the country.

Illegal Encroachments

The unlawful seizure and occupation of public land and unauthorized construction projects are a significant challenge. These illegal encroachments often lead to a loss of public resources and can disrupt urban planning and environmental balance. We work to expose these activities and advocate for legal action to reclaim public property and restore order. Our efforts help ensure that public spaces are protected for the benefit of all citizens.

Empowering Citizens

A well-informed citizenry is the most effective defense against legal and social injustices. We believe in empowering people with knowledge about their rights and the laws of the land. Our team conducts a variety of educational initiatives, including training camps and workshops on legal activism, awareness drives, and training programs. These programs are designed to help citizens understand their constitutional rights and national laws, enabling them to identify illegal activities and take informed action.
